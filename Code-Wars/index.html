WELCOME
To CODE WARS SEASON 1


We're thrilled to bring together the brightest minds in technology for an immersive 24-hour hackathon at GND University. This is a unique opportunity to collaborate, create, and innovate within the energizing environment of an offline event. Over two 12-hour sprints, participants will form teams, brainstorm ideas, and build prototypes and MVPs. By working closely side-by-side, you can maximize in-person interactions to bring concepts to life.
Our hackathon provides the space, community, and support participants need to excel:

  • Dedicated work areas across the GND campus to promote collaboration.
  • Mentors and subject-matter experts available in person for guidance.
  • Food, coffee/chai, and snacks to power you through the evening challenge.
  • Prizes for top projects in various categories.
  • Entry ticket costs Rs.300
At the end of the 24 hours, teams will demo their projects in front of judges and attendees. Winners will be announced at the closing ceremony. We welcome diverse thinkers, skill sets, and perspectives. Join us to experience the energy of an offline hackathon!...

Rules And Info

1) Team Size: Assemble a team of 3 or 4 persons.
2) It is going for two days of 12-12 hour event. Enjoy breakfast and lunch on us each day.
3) Concise and Catchy: The title should be short and memorable, capturing the essence of the event.
4) Inspirational: Use words that inspire creativity, innovation, and participation.
5) Discipline: No student is allowed to Leave the UIT Building of University.
6) Make sure to carry your water bottles, laptop, charger and extensions(if possible) with you.
7) Entry Ticket: Rs.300
